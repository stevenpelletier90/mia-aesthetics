What is an Eyelid Lift?

Eyelid lift surgery, also known as blepharoplasty, is a popular and effective procedure that rejuvenates the eye area.

Corrects issues like:

  • Double eyelid
  • Puffy under-eye bags
  • Blocked peripheral vision
  • Eyelid damage
  • Difficulty applying eye makeup
THE PROCEDURE

About the Eyelid Lift Procedure

Performed under general anesthesia:

  • Upper eyelid: Incision along natural crease
  • Lower eyelid: Incision below lash line or inside eyelid
  • Removes/redistributes excess skin, muscle, and fat
  • Closes incision with sutures, surgical tape, or adhesives
HEALING PROCESS

Eyelid Lift/Blepharoplasty Recovery

Initial blurry vision and light sensitivity. Swelling and bruising around eyes. Avoid rubbing eyes and contact lenses for 2 weeks. Pain medication prescribed.

Preliminary results in 6-8 weeks. Final results around 6 months post-surgery.

RESULTS

Is an Eyelid Lift Permanent?

Lower eyelid surgery is permanent. Upper eyelid may start drooping 5-7 years later.

CANDIDACY

Candidate Requirements

Must be in excellent physical/mental health, with realistic expectations, and not:

  • Pregnant
  • Recently had facial surgery
  • Taking blood thinners
  • Suffering from blood diseases or autoimmune disorders
