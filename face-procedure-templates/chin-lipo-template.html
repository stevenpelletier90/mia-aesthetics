What is Chin Lipo?

Chin lipo removes excess fat from beneath the chin. This minimally invasive procedure provides a defined jawline and permanently removes fat cells in the targeted area.

The procedure enhances jawline definition, boosts self-confidence, and provides long-lasting results through a safe, minimally invasive approach.

About the Chin Lipo Procedure

The procedure takes 30-60 minutes and can be performed under general or local anesthesia (awake liposuction). The surgical technique follows these steps:

  1. Small incision is made under the chin or behind the ears
  2. Special fluid is injected to prepare fat for removal
  3. A cannula is used to remove fat cells
  4. The area is sculpted into the desired shape

This minimally invasive approach ensures precision while maintaining safety throughout the procedure.

Chin Lipo Recovery

Mild swelling and bruising are expected after the procedure. Patients will need to wear a compression garment for 5-7 days to support healing and reduce swelling.

Most patients can return to work within 1-2 weeks, depending on their healing progress and job requirements. Final results become visible in 1-3 months as all swelling subsides.

The recovery process is generally well-tolerated, with most patients experiencing minimal discomfort during the healing period.

Ideal Candidates for Chin Lipo

Good candidates for chin liposuction typically have:

  • Good overall health
  • Good skin elasticity
  • No significant medical complications
  • Realistic expectations about results
  • Non-smoking status (preferred)

Important: Candidates should be at or near their ideal weight for optimal results.

Results and Longevity

Frequently asked questions about chin lipo results:

  • Results are considered permanent if weight is maintained
  • Fat cells removed during the procedure do not return
  • Weight gain can create new fat cells in the area
  • Final results visible in 1-3 months

The procedure provides long-lasting enhancement to jawline definition and facial profile when combined with a stable weight.
