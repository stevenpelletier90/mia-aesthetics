What Is Chin Lipo?

Removes excess fat from beneath the chin. Minimally invasive procedure. Provides a dramatic new jawline contour. Permanently removes fat cells in the targeted area.

BENEFITS

Why Choose Chin Lipo?

Enhances jawline definition. Boosts self-confidence. Safe and minimally invasive. Provides natural, long-lasting results.

THE PROCEDURE

How the Surgery Works

Procedure takes 30-60 minutes. Can be done under general or local anesthesia. Surgeon makes small incision under chin or behind ears. Uses special fluid to prepare fat for removal. Thin tube (cannula) removes fat cells. Sculpts area into more pleasing shape.

CANDIDACY

Who Is a Good Candidate for Chin Lipo?

Should be free of significant medical issues. Requires good skin elasticity. Not recommended for those with uncontrolled medical conditions. Smokers must quit before procedure. May require alternative procedures like neck lift for severe skin laxity.

HEALING PROCESS

Recovery and What to Expect After Surgery

Mild swelling, bruising, and tightness expected. Wear compression garment for 5-7 days. Avoid strenuous activity for two weeks. Final results visible in 1-3 months. Most patients return to normal activities within 1-2 weeks.
