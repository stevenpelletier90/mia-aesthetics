What Is Chin Lipo?

It happens suddenly—you're just going along living your life and then one day you look in the mirror and it's there. The dreaded double chin has appeared! Fortunately, there is no need to get upset. Your next step is looking into chin liposuction.

Called chin lipo for short, this procedure removes excess fat from beneath your chin. The process is minimally invasive but provides maximum results, delivering a dramatic new jawline contour. Perhaps the best news is that the fat cells removed during the procedure are gone for good. So long as you maintain a stable weight, your new chin will stay with you for many happy years.

BENEFITS

Why Choose Chin Lipo?

You want your double chin removed, and chin lipo is the most effective way to banish it. The procedure offers many benefits, including:

  • Enhanced jawline definition and contouring
  • A boost in self-confidence (you won't be needing those scarves anymore)
  • A safe, minimally invasive procedure with a fast recovery
  • Natural, long-lasting results
THE PROCEDURE

How the Surgery Works

Jawline contouring with chin liposuction is a fairly short surgery, taking only about 30 to 60 minutes. You may choose to be under general anesthesia for your procedure or opt for awake liposuction, which uses a local anesthetic to keep you comfortable. At Mia Aesthetics, our experienced plastic surgeons perform chin lipo in a sterile environment using advanced techniques that provide extreme precision.

During chin lipo, your surgeon makes a small incision under your chin or behind your ears. The chin area is flooded with a special fluid that plumps the fat for easier removal and further numbs the area. A thin tube called a cannula is then passed through the incision and used to remove excess, unwanted fat cells. After sculpting the area into a more pleasing shape, your surgeon closes your incision(s) and sends you home to rest and recover.

CANDIDACY

Who Is a Good Candidate for Chin Lipo?

Should be free of significant medical issues. Requires good skin elasticity. Not recommended for those with uncontrolled medical conditions. Smokers must quit before procedure. May require alternative procedures like neck lift for severe skin laxity.

HEALING PROCESS

Recovery and What to Expect After Surgery

Immediately after your jawline contouring, you will notice some mild swelling, bruising, and tightness around your chin and jawline. This is expected and not cause for alarm. As part of your post-op care, your plastic surgeon will require that you:

  • Take any pain medication as prescribed.
  • Eat soft foods if chewing is uncomfortable for you.
  • Keep any follow-up appointments.
  • Wear a compression garment for five to seven days to reduce swelling.
  • Keep your incisions clean and dry.
  • Avoid strenuous activity for at least two weeks.

Most patients can return to work and other normal daily activities within one to two weeks of the procedure. Your final results will become visible in about one to three months as the last of the residual swelling subsides.
