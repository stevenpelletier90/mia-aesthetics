What Is a Mini Facelift?

A mini facelift is a surgical procedure that reduces signs of aging for a more youthful, rejuvenated look. This particular surgery focuses on the lower portion of the face and jawline, allowing for fewer incisions and a faster recovery than a traditional facelift. It's also less expensive.

THE PROCESS

The Procedure and Recovery Process

We know that although surgery is just part of the daily routine for us, it's anything but ordinary for you. Many of our patients appreciate knowing what to expect every step of the way.

Your care team will monitor you as you wake up from the anesthesia and then send you home the same day as your surgery. We will prescribe pain medication to help with any discomfort you may have.

Your surgeon will schedule a follow-up appointment with you a few days after your surgery. It's important that you keep this appointment so that your surgeon can check on your healing, remove any stitches, and make sure you don't have any complications.
