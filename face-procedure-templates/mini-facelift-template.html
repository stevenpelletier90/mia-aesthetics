What Is a Mini Facelift?

A mini facelift is a surgical procedure that reduces signs of aging for a more youthful, rejuvenated look. This particular surgery focuses on the lower portion of the face and jawline, allowing for fewer incisions and a faster recovery than a traditional facelift. It's also less expensive.

Unlike dermal fillers, Botox, laser treatments, and other skin rejuvenation options, a mini facelift can easily last five to 10 years and doesn't require ongoing treatments.

Who Is a Good Candidate?

Ideal candidates for a mini facelift are people who meet the following criteria:

  • Are typically (but not always) in their 40s and 50s
  • Display good skin elasticity
  • Live an overall healthy lifestyle that will promote good healing and a successful outcome (i.e. nonsmokers)
  • Have realistic expectations about what the surgery can do

The Procedure and Recovery Process

  1. Free virtual consultation to assess candidacy
  2. Day of surgery: Preparation and general anesthesia
  3. Surgeon makes hidden incisions around the ear
  4. Separate skin from underlying tissue and muscles
  5. Tighten muscles as needed
  6. Remove excess skin
  7. Smooth remaining skin over lower face
  8. Close incisions and apply dressings

Patients will be monitored after surgery and sent home the same day. Pain medication will be prescribed, and patients should manage swelling with ice packs. A follow-up appointment will be scheduled to remove stitches and check healing.

Mini Facelift vs. Traditional Facelift

Mini Facelift

  • Focuses on lower face and jawline
  • Fewer incisions required
  • Faster recovery time
  • Less expensive
  • Results last 5-10 years
  • Same-day outpatient procedure

Traditional Facelift

  • Addresses entire face
  • More extensive incisions
  • Longer recovery period
  • Higher cost
  • Longer-lasting results
  • More comprehensive rejuvenation
