What is a Circumferential Body Lift?

A circumferential body lift, also known as a lower body lift or a belt lipectomy, is a comprehensive cosmetic surgery that aims to reshape and tighten the mid and lower body by removing excess skin and fat.

COMPARISON

Circumferential Body Lift Vs. Tummy Tuck

During a tummy tuck, the surgeon removes excess skin from the abdomen only. A lower body lift removes excess skin and fat from the entire midsection, including back, outer thighs, and buttocks.

COMPARISON

Circumferential Body Lift Vs. Liposuction

A lower body lift removes both excess fat and skin, while liposuction only removes fat. Liposuction is more subtle and can be performed in various body areas.

THE PROCEDURE

How a Circumferential Body Lift Works

The procedure involves:

  • General anesthesia
  • Making an incision around the body's circumference
  • Cutting away excess skin and fat
  • Tightening muscles
  • Lifting and suturing remaining skin
  • Placing patient in a compression garment
CANDIDACY

Are You a Good Candidate?

Candidates should:

  • Have lost significant weight
  • Maintain stable weight
  • Be in good health
  • Not smoke
  • Have no pregnancy plans
  • Commit to a healthy lifestyle
