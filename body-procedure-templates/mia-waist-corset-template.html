What is Rib Repositioning?

The Mia Waist Corset™ is a rib repositioning procedure that changes the position of the bottom ribs without removing them to create a more defined waistline.

Starting Cost: $5,500 - Takes an additional 30 to 60 minutes during surgery.

SAFETY

Safer and More Effective Than Rib Removal

Rib repositioning provides comparable results without the long-term complications of rib removal. Your waistline will be greatly improved, but your ribs will still be there to protect your internal organs and stabilize your core.

BENEFITS

Benefits of Rib Repositioning

Benefits include:

  • Reshaping waist contour
  • Increasing impact of other body procedures
  • Maintaining rib protection for internal organs
  • Reducing risk of chronic pain
  • Fewer complications than rib removal
CANDIDACY

Who is a Good Candidate for Rib Repositioning?

Good candidates are:

  • Already undergoing a lipo 360, BBL, or tummy tuck procedure
  • Looking for additional waist contouring
  • In good overall health
  • Nonsmokers
  • Have realistic expectations
THE PROCEDURE

How Rib Repositioning Works

The procedure involves:

  • Accessing floating ribs through small incisions
  • Carefully dissecting muscles and soft tissue
  • Precisely fracturing and repositioning ribs
  • Using sutures or wires to hold ribs in new position
