What is a Mommy Makeover?

You adore your children, but you may not feel the same about the ways your body has changed since having them.

A surgical procedure addressing body and skin changes after pregnancy. Can include multiple procedures customized to individual needs. Recommended for women who are done having children.

PROCEDURES

Which specific procedures can be included in a mommy makeover?

Potential procedures include:

  • Breast Augmentation
  • Breast Lift
  • Tummy Tuck
  • Liposuction
  • Brazilian Butt Lift (BBL)
HEALING PROCESS

Mommy Makeover Recovery

Generally takes 1-4 weeks to return to work. Patients can typically:

  • Drive after one week
  • Walk five miles on flat surface in two weeks
  • Run after four weeks

Requires support for daily activities during initial recovery. Involves using compression garments and prescribed medication.
