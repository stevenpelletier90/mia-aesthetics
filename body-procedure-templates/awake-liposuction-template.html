What is Awake Lipo?

Awake liposuction is a minimally invasive procedure where the patient is awake during the surgery.

THE PROCEDURE

How Does Awake Lipo Work?

Awake liposuction works similarly to traditional liposuction, but the patient remains conscious. The surgeon uses:

  • Sedatives and local anesthesia
  • A tumescent solution to numb the area
  • A cannula to remove unwanted fat
  • Strategic fat removal to sculpt the body
BENEFITS

What are the benefits of Awake Lipo?

Benefits include:

  • Surgeon can communicate with patient during procedure
  • Potentially better body sculpting results
  • Fewer risks compared to general anesthesia
  • Faster recovery
  • Less post-surgical grogginess
  • Easier fat removal using tumescent fluid
TREATMENT AREAS

What Parts of the Body Can Be Treated?

Treatment areas include:

  • Abdomen
  • Arms
  • Armpits
  • Ankles
  • Back (upper, mid, lower)
  • Chin
  • Hips
  • Knees
  • Neck
  • Thighs
  • And more
CANDIDACY

Am I a Good Candidate for Awake Lipo?

Best candidates are:

  • Non-smokers
  • Generally healthy
  • Close to ideal body weight
  • Potentially eligible with higher BMI
