What is Liposuction?

Liposuction is a procedure that sculpts areas of the body with excess fat, streamlining, slimming and contouring the body.

THE PROCEDURE

About the Liposuction Procedure

Performed under general anesthesia. Small incisions made in treatment areas. Uses tumescent technique with anesthetic fluid. A cannula (small hollow tube) is inserted to loosen and suction fat. Surgeon carefully contours treated areas.

HEALING PROCESS

Liposuction Recovery

Patient fitted with compression garment. Temporary drains may be used (removed in 7-10 days). Most patients can return to desk jobs within a week. Follow surgeon's instructions for optimal healing.
