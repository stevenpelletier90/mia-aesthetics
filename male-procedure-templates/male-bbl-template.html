How Male BBL Works

The Male BBL process is similar to the female BBL procedure:

  1. Surgeon performs liposuction to remove excess fat from specific body areas
  2. Fat is washed, spun, and purified
  3. Fat is strategically injected into buttocks to create desired shape and size

The procedure uses the patient's own natural body fat, ensuring results that look and feel natural with no artificial implants.
