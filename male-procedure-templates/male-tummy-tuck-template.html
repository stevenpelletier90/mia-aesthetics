Male Tummy Tuck

Male tummy tuck is designed to eliminate stubborn belly fat for men and focuses on removing excess fat and loose skin that won't respond to exercise.

Starting Price: $4,600*

Key Procedure Differences for Men

  1. No muscle wall repair (unlike women's tummy tucks)
  2. Specific focus on belly button placement
  3. Fat removal aimed at revealing potential six-pack definition
  4. Straight incision line versus curved for women

Important Note: A tummy tuck won't automatically create a six-pack - that requires dedicated gym work.

Procedure Combinations

Male tummy tuck can be combined with other procedures such as:

  • Liposuction
  • Lipo 360
  • Facelift
  • Gynecomastia repair
  • Arm lifts
  • Thigh lifts

Candidate Qualifications

  • Generally healthy
  • Non-smoker
  • Close to ideal body weight
  • Chronic conditions must be well-controlled

Good candidates are men who have excess skin and fat in the abdominal area that doesn't respond to diet and exercise alone.

Recovery Timeline

  • Full recovery: Up to 6 months
  • Return to normal life: 2-3 weeks
  • Return to work: About 2 weeks
  • Return to vigorous activity: 4-5 weeks

Recovery times vary by individual and the extent of the procedure. Following post-operative instructions is crucial for optimal healing and results.
