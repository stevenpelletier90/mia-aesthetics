Comprehensive typography system using Montserrat for headings and Inter for body text, with responsive clamp() scaling.

Body Text

Lead paragraph - larger text for introductions and important content that needs emphasis. Perfect for hero sections and article intros.

Regular paragraph text - the standard body text used throughout the site. Optimized for readability with proper line spacing and comfortable reading length. This text should be easily scannable and accessible.

Small text for captions, disclaimers, and secondary information that supports the main content.
