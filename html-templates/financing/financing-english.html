How Payments Work

STEP 1

Research Surgeons

  • Choose from over 25 experienced surgeons with diverse specialties and locations
  • Review surgeon bios on our website to learn more
  • Browse our before and after photos
STEP 2

Check Specials

  • Contact a sales coordinator to learn about available specials
  • New surgeons often offer reduced rates
  • Keep an eye on seasonal specials for the best deals
STEP 3

Lock in Your Price

  • Call us to secure a special price before it expires
  • A booking fee is required to lock in the price, which stays valid for 12 months (this can be paid with Visa, MasterCard, Discover, American Express credit cards, or a Money Order)
  • Schedule your surgery and complete payments within this period
STEP 4

Complete Your Payments

  • Make payments at your own pace, ensuring the full amount is paid 10 days before surgery
  • Contact your patient concierge via phone or email for payments or assistance
  • If needed, ask your concierge for a contract extension to get more time
