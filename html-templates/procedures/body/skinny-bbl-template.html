What is a Skinny BBL?

A skinny BBL is a specialized Brazilian butt lift designed for leaner individuals with less body fat. As in a traditional BBL, the surgeon extracts donor fat using liposuction and then meticulously injects it into the buttocks to create a more desirable shape. The procedure enhances the buttocks while maintaining a natural look that complements the patient's slender frame. Because of the limited fat available, achieving the desired results may require a more strategic approach.

How Skinny BBL Surgery Works

Every person is different, so the specific details of every surgery are different as well. Generally, however, a skinny BBL occurs as follows:

  1. You will have a detailed consultation with a surgeon or surgical coordinator to create a fat harvesting plan.
  2. Your care team will prep you for surgery and place you under general anesthesia on the day of surgery.
  3. Liposuction will be performed to harvest fat from areas like the abdomen, flanks, lower back, thighs, and sometimes arms.
  4. The harvested fat will be purified to ensure only the highest quality fat cells are used.
  5. Your surgeon will precisely inject the purified fat into specific buttocks areas to enhance shape, size, and contour using ultrasound technology.
  6. We will monitor you after your procedure until you are ready to go home.

Skinny BBL Weight, BMI, and Other Requirements

There is no minimum weight requirement for a skinny BBL. It matters only that you have adequate fat for harvesting and transfer. In general, a skinny BBL requires that you be or have:

  • A BMI of 25 or less
  • Adequate donor fat
  • Able to maintain a stable weight
  • Good overall health
  • A nonsmoker
  • Realistic expectations
  • Adequate commitment to recovery
